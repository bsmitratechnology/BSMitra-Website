About BS Mitra Technology

BS Mitra Technology was founded with a simple vision: to empower individuals, students, and small businesses by making world-class digital services accessible and affordable.

Based in the heart of Odisha, our startup focuses on:

  • Designing fast and responsive websites
  • Developing reliable Android applications
  • Offering internship training and live projects to students
  • Providing personalized IT consultation for local businesses

At BS Mitra, we don't just build software — we build relationships. Every client, intern, or collaborator becomes a part of our journey toward a more digitally enabled future.
